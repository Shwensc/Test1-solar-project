Harnessing the Power Of Solar And Renewable Energy

The project helps homeowners estimate how many solar panels can fit on their rooftops using satellite imagery. It also calculates the potential energy they could generate from installing those panels.

Main idea of Project

Develop a geospatial web based portal for estimating the power that can be generated using solar energy based on the size of civilian housing rooftops. The system should determine how much solar energy can be generated at building level using existing solar radiation related satellite data.
